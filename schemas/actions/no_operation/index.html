No Operation

An action that performs no operation (a 'noop'). It simply passes its input directly to its output. Useful as a placeholder or for debugging workflow paths.

Overview

The No Operation action is the simplest action in the SoEasily toolkit - it does absolutely nothing except pass data through unchanged. While this might seem trivial, it serves important purposes in workflow development: acting as a placeholder during design, creating connection points for complex routing, enabling debugging and testing, and maintaining workflow structure during refactoring.
